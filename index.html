About Sample Sequencer

Sample Sequencer is a free online drum machine and beat maker that runs entirely in your browser — no downloads, no signup. Pick sounds from the built-in drum kit library, upload your own audio files, or record new sounds with your microphone. Trim each clip, add effects like reverb, delay, distortion, compression, chorus, phaser, and vibrato, then arrange them across four 16-step patterns. When you are happy with the beat, download it as a .wav file to use in any DAW or share with others.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Sample Sequencer free?
Yes. Sample Sequencer is completely free to use with no signup or account required.
Do I need to download or install anything?
No. The app runs entirely in your browser. It works in Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari.
Can I upload my own samples?
Yes. Use the upload button in the sampler to load any audio file from your computer (such as .wav or .mp3) and sequence it like any other sample.
Can I record audio with my microphone?
Yes. Click the record button in the sampler to capture audio from your microphone, then trim and add effects before sequencing it.
What audio effects are included?
Each sample supports gain, compression, distortion, delay, and reverb. The full track adds chorus, phaser, and vibrato on top of gain, compression, and reverb.
Can I download my finished beat?
Yes. Once your track is ready, click the download button to export it as a .wav file you can use in any DAW or share.
How many patterns can I sequence?
The sequencer has four 16-step patterns. Patterns that contain notes are played back in order on loop.
Does it work on mobile?
The interface is responsive, but the sequencer grid is best used on a desktop or tablet for comfortable note placement.
